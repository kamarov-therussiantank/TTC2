    What is TTCV2?

TTCV2 is a Chrome extension inspired by the classic TankTrouble game. It aims to bring back the essence of the original TankTrouble Classic but in a fresh way. With TTCV2, I'm carefully restoring many beloved features from the old TankTrouble version. This project is all about reviving the visuals that made Tank Trouble memorable, along with UI improvements and styles that enhance the overall experience. Expect a mix of classic graphics and textures that seamlessly blend together. As I continue developing TTCV2, you can look forward to exciting new features that elevate your gaming experience.

    What is the goal of TTCV2?

My objective is straightforward: to restore as much content as feasible from the original game version to the newer iteration. While achieving a full restoration may prove challenging, the endeavor is certainly worth pursuing. Despite potential limitations, I remain committed to the pursuit of preserving and enhancing the gaming experience."
