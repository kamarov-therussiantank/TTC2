    What is TTCV2?

TTCV2 is a Chrome extension. It aims to bring back the essence of the original TankTrouble Classic but in a fresh way. With TTCV2, I'm carefully restoring many beloved features from the old TankTrouble version (aka Classic TankTrouble). This project is all about reviving the visuals that made Tank Trouble memorable, along with look and feel that enhance the overall experience. As I continue developing TTCV2, you can look forward to exciting new features that elevate your gaming experience.

    What is the goal of TTCV2?

My objective is straightforward: to restore as much content as feasible from the old game version to the newer. While achieving a full restoration may prove challenging, the endeavor is certainly worth pursuing. Despite potential limitations, I remain committed to the pursuit of preserving and enhancing the gaming experience.

    Origin of TTCV2?

TTCV2 is based on the old version of TankTrouble (aka Classic TankTrouble), that is no longer available to people. Since its shutdown, people started to miss the old days spend in it, same applies for me. I couldn't just sit there and watch how Classic TankTrouble is getting forsaken. I had to do something about it. And thats the time when the idea of making this extension started.
